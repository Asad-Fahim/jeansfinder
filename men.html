How to Style Skinny Jeans

  • Dark washes look more sleek and modern - great for evenings out.
  • Balance the slim bottoms with a slightly looser top or jacket.
  • Chelsea boots or clean white sneakers work perfectly with this style.
  • You should be able to pinch a little fabric - if they're too tight, size up!
  • Look for styles with some stretch if you have athletic thighs.
